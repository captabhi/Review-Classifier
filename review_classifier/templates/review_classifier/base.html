About

Karachi Bakery is located on Moazzam Jahi Market, Hyderabad, in the Indian state of Telangana. It was founded by Sri Khanchand Ramnani ji. It is one of the popular bakeries in Hyderabad. It is known for its fruit biscuits, dil kush and plum cake.
